## House and landscape
Casa Alborea is organized as a sequence of residential pavilions around courtyards, gardens and terraces. Stone walls, clear planes and broad glass openings create a continuous relationship between the social spaces, the pool and the interior landscape.

## Technical development
CR Collective focused on coordinated BIM modeling, building-systems documentation and exterior visualization. The package connects the architectural criteria with air-conditioning, hydraulic, sanitary, gas, lighting, data and rainwater diagrams.
