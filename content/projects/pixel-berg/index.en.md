## Urban counterpoint
Pixel-Berg is an academic study developed in Madrid. The proposal sits among the five towers of Paseo de la Castellana and uses a horizontal, stepped and porous block as a counterpoint to the vertical financial district.

## Collective program
The project brings together university classrooms, a lobby, a café, temporary housing for patients' companions and a 200-seat experimental theater. Its ground floor operates as shared terrain, connecting the different programs to the existing public space.

## Section and movement
The form grows from a sequence of platforms, cantilevers and cores that organize circulation in section. Early sketches test a folded mass that responds to the site's irregular edges and opens views between the towers.
