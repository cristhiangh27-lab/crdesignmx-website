Design + Execution + Supervision

CR Collective

We are a collaborative architecture and visualization studio. We design and coordinate contemporary projects with BIM methodology to ensure coherence between concept, documentation, and execution. We operate as a work network with architects and civil engineers, integrating structure and MEP to deliver buildable, efficient solutions aligned with budget. Our approach: clear scope, orderly processes, and constant communication with the client.

Mission

Create contemporary spaces that respond to real needs through transparent processes, constant communication, and informed decisions.

Values

  • Claridad en alcances, costos y entregables.
  • Colaboración con clientes y consultores especializados.
  • Responsabilidad técnica apoyada en BIM y documentación precisa.
  • Diseño sensible al contexto, clima y operación futura.
